## The idea
A practical, no-fluff downloadable PDF guide for aspiring pet-waste removal entrepreneurs. Draws directly on Silas's own experience running Humboldt Scoop: how to price routes, acquire customers, handle equipment, schedule efficiently, and survive bad-weather days. Sells as a pdf-manual product on Gumroad or Etsy. Low production cost since the knowledge already exists — it just needs to be organized and written.

## Why it's worth doing
The niche is real and underserved: pet waste removal is a low-capital service business with proven demand but almost no dedicated "how to start" resources beyond generic entrepreneurship advice. Silas has first-hand operational experience. The humboldt-scoop-cms tooling being built can be featured as a bonus resource, creating cross-promotion between projects. Revenue potential: $15–$25 per sale with near-zero COGS.
