# Pitch: Coastal Creature Coloring Zine

date: 2026-06-26
project-target: digital-storefront
status: passed

## The idea
Make a short PDF coloring zine of whimsical Humboldt coast creatures doing tiny useful jobs: tidepool janitors, fog-herding cats, redwood mushroom librarians, and harbor-seal mail carriers. The tone should be cozy, queer-positive, all-ages, and easy to expand into themed volumes.

## Why it's worth doing
It fits the approved `pdf-coloring` product type, needs no new platform or backend work, and can be drafted as black-and-white line-art prompts plus page descriptions before any images are generated. It also gives the storefront pipeline a friendly starter product that feels more like Kind Robots than generic POD slop. Tiny goblin moat achieved.
