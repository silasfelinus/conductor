# Pitch: Kind Robots AI Character Coloring Pack (PDF)
date: 2026-06-30
project-target: digital-storefront
status: awaiting-silas

## The idea
A downloadable PDF coloring book (20–30 pages) featuring original characters from the Kind Robots universe — Bots, Characters, and Scenarios — rendered as clean line-art for coloring. Art is generated via the existing kind_robots Stable Diffusion pipeline using a line-art ControlNet preset, then lightly cleaned and laid out in a standard 8.5"×11" format. Sells as a pdf-coloring product on Gumroad, Etsy, and potentially Amazon KDP.

## Why it's worth doing
Kind Robots already has art generation infrastructure and a growing cast of characters. This pitch converts that existing creative capital into a tangible sellable product. It also creates a natural brand touchpoint: buyers see the character names and world, potentially converting to Kind Robots users. Coloring books have low price sensitivity and high perceived value as a gift item. Approved product type (pdf-coloring) — no new-type pitch required.

## Suggested first task
Select 10 existing Kind Robots characters/Bots, generate line-art variants via A1111 with a coloring-book ControlNet style, assess print quality, and produce a 5-page sample PDF. If quality is acceptable, proceed to full 25-page layout. Needs Silas approval on art before finalizing.
