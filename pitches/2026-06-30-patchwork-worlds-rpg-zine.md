# Pitch: Patchwork Worlds — One-Shot TTRPG Adventure Zine
date: 2026-06-30
project-target: digital-storefront
status: awaiting-silas

## The idea
A rules-light tabletop RPG adventure zine containing 3 self-contained one-shot scenarios designed for 2–4 players and a GM with zero prep. Setting is "patchwork portal fantasy" — travelers pulled from different worlds who must cooperate across culture and language barriers to solve a problem and get home. Tone: queer-positive, low combat, character-driven, hopeful. System-agnostic with stat blocks for one popular free system (likely Cairn or Mörk Borg Lite). Sells as pdf-rpg on itch.io and Gumroad.

## Why it's worth doing
Rules-light one-shots are the best-selling RPG PDF format on itch.io — low buy-in for customers, no ongoing commitment, playable in a single session. The patchwork genre and character-cooperation hook are highly differentiated from grimdark competitors. Genre guidance from CONTROL.md aligns exactly (RPG: rules-light, GM-friendly; queer-positive, hopeful). Three tight scenarios fit a single-issue zine production run. Approved product type (pdf-rpg). First step toward a potential series.

medium

## Suggested first task
Write a one-page premise for each of the 3 scenarios (setup, central conflict, three possible endings) and a brief world primer (1 page). Silas reviews and chooses which scenario to develop fully first. No mechanics written until scenario structure is locked.
