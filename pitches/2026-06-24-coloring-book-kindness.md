# Pitch: "Acts of Kindness" coloring book (PDF + POD)
date: 2026-06-24
project-target: digital-storefront
status: approved

## The idea
A coloring book where each page illustrates a small act of kindness — feeding a stray,
planting a tree, helping a neighbor — with a one-line prompt to do that act this week.
Ties directly to the Kind Robots mission. Sell as a digital PDF and as print-on-demand.

## Why it's worth doing
Uses two already-approved product types (pdf-coloring, pod-text-art), needs no new
infrastructure, and reinforces the brand's social-good angle. Low effort, on-mission,
testable as a first storefront product.
