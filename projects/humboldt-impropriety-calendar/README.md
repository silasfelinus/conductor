# Humboldt Impropriety Calendar

A consent-first custom-calendar project documenting Humboldt County's adult play-party and performance history from Club Risqué through Reasons to Say Yes and Humboldt Impropriety Society.

This project is mature, historical, community-centered, and public-safe by default. Private archival performance videos may inform research only. They must not be published, uploaded, used in previews, or shared outside explicit written consent.

## Product frame

- Product type: `custom-calendar`
- First issue: Humboldt Impropriety Calendar
- Public posture: safe-ish-for-work previews, historical framing, clear 18+ context where appropriate
- Private posture: strict consent, credit approval, payout approval, and archive boundaries

## Contributor payout baseline

For a 12-month calendar, the default royalty pool is 12/20 of net profit:

- 20 total project shares
- 12 month shares allocated to contributors
- 1/20 net profit per month
- 8/20 retained for project/admin, design, fulfillment overhead, support, samples, contingency, and reinvestment

Each month's 1/20 share can be split by preset or custom signed agreement among approved model(s), artist(s), photographer(s), director(s), and historical contributors.

## Hard boundaries

No contributor outreach, public listing, vendor upload, payment setup, printing, publication, or public use of private footage happens without explicit human approval.
