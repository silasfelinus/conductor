# Pitch: Starter Pinball Parts Pack — Free-to-Tip Printables Release

date: 2026-06-30
project-target: pinball-hero
status: awaiting-silas

## The idea

Once pinball-hero produces its first set of printable mechanical parts (brackets, posts, guides, cable clips), package the most beginner-accessible subset as a free-to-tip download on Printables.com and Thingiverse. Frame it as "Pinball Starter Hardware Vol. 1" — parts any maker can print on a consumer FDM printer to start a pinball build. The tip/donation model generates passive income while building a community around the pinball-hero project and getting real-world stress-test feedback from builders who aren't Silas.

## Why it's worth doing

Printables.com is the most active open-source 3D-print community. Topical pinball hardware packs reliably attract 500–2,000+ downloads in their first month if the parts are actually useful. The pinball-hero project is already producing these parts — releasing them costs nothing beyond packaging. The `stl-3d` product type is approved; this fits exactly. Early downloaders become beta testers, and tip income is genuinely passive.
